Refund Policy

BuildClarity Private Limited — Wadala, Mumbai, India

Consumer Protection Act 2019 Effective Date: June 2025

At BuildClarity Private Limited, we invest significant time and expertise from the moment a project is confirmed. Please read this policy carefully before engaging our services.

1. General Principle

Since our services are knowledge-based and time-intensive , refunds are evaluated based on stage of work completed at time of cancellation. No blanket full refunds once work begins.

2. Cancellation Fee

10% Non-Refundable Cancellation Fee

Applies to ALL cancellations regardless of reason or stage. Covers administrative costs, discussions, and resource allocation.

3. Refund Schedule

Stage of Cancellation Refund Eligibility
Before work starts (within 24 hours of advance, no files shared) 90% of advance refunded (10% cancellation fee deducted)
After work started, before 50% completion 50% of advance refunded (10% cancellation fee deducted)
After 50%+ work completed No refund on advance
After final deliverables shared No refund applicable
Client unresponsive 30+ days after advance payment No refund — project abandoned

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • ❌ Final deliverables already shared
  • ❌ Client changed mind after work commenced
  • ❌ Dissatisfaction due to inaccurate info provided by Client
  • ❌ Client failed to respond during project
  • ❌ Delays caused by Client leading to rework
  • ❌ Dissatisfaction with third-party contractor outcomes
  • ❌ Force majeure situations
