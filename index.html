Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How much does a municipality-approved home plan cost?
Our plans start at just ₹4,999 for basic 2D floor plans. The Premium plan at ₹9,999 includes 3D elevation, structural drawings, and municipality-ready documents. This saves you ₹10,000-₹15,000 compared to traditional architect fees.
How long does it take to get my home plan?
Basic plans are delivered in 5 working days. Premium plans take 7 days. Ultimate plans take 10 days. Revisions are completed within 1-2 days.
Will this plan be approved by my local municipality?
Yes! All our plans are designed to comply with local building bylaws and municipality requirements. We follow nagar nigam guidelines specific to your city and include all required drawings and documents for approval.
Do you provide structural drawings for building permits?
Yes, our Premium and Ultimate plans include complete structural drawings — column layout, beam details, foundation plan, slab design, and reinforcement details. All designed by a licensed civil engineer.
Which cities do you serve?
We serve homeowners across all cities in India. Since our work is delivered digitally, location is not a barrier. We customize plans according to your local municipality's specific requirements.
Can I get revisions if I don't like the design?
Absolutely! Basic plan includes 1 free revision, Premium includes 3 revisions, and Ultimate offers unlimited revisions. We work until you're 100% satisfied with your design.
